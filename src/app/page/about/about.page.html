ABOUT US

Touchline is a small and developing Hookah shop and a delivery servive
located at Soshanguve that aims at allowing customers to be able to order hookah or it's materials
at the comfort of their own homes in just simple steps from their own devices.

You can place orders with the shop from the Touchline website or mobile app,
and your products will be delivered directly to your door, usually for a small delivery fee in and around
Soshanguve, Mabopane and Garankuwa.
